Introduction

ViA International is an internationally recognized consultancy firm specialized in civil engineering design and project management. With more than 35 years of experience, the group provides services in all aspects of civil engineering including architectural, transport, civil infrastructures, structural and hydraulic engineering, MEP, land and building development, marinas, harbors, railways, as well as advanced specialties and interactive 3D modeling.

This proposal outlines an AI-based tender management solution that will automate and streamline the entire tender submission process. The solution will leverage artificial intelligence to identify opportunities, extract key information, create customized documentation, and manage the approval workflow with minimal human intervention.

Key Benefits

  • Automated tender opportunity identification
  • Intelligent eligibility assessment
  • Streamlined approval process
  • AI-generated tender documentation
  • Customized CV generation
  • Multi-language support
  • Time and resource savings
  • Increased tender participation capacity
